FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Besides this FAQ section, we invite you to read our T&Cs and groups guidelines. They exist to make sure that we start a relationship of mutual understanding and to make sure that Nucapacha is a safe, happy and welcoming place to stay. By confirming a reservation at Hostel Nucapacha, regardless if this sheet is signed, you are agreeing to all important points described.

Where is the hostel located? Is it close to the city center?

We are located in Urdesa Central, this is 15 to 20 minutes away from what is known as the city center. Due to the exorbitant growing of Guayaquil, Urdesa is the geographical center of the city and what you may know as the ‘centro’ would actually be the geographical east. Urdesa is a beautiful upscale neighborhood that lies on the estuary side, in spite of the commercial activity on the main streets, the mayor has successfully achieved that the side streets remain a residential area. We are two blocks away from the main avenue Victor Emilio Estrada, where you can find many banks, restaurants, retail stores, bars, and office buildings.

How do I get from the bus terminal/airport to the hostel

Public transportation in Ecuador can be a little bit messy and although we are sure after a few days you’ll become an expert, we highly recommend that for getting here you take a taxi. It’s easier and safer is you are with all your bags. Check out the hostel location pages for directions.

Are you close to public transportation?

We are only 2 blocks away from one of the main avenues in the city were you can get access to a lot of buses that will drive you pretty much everywhere in the city.

Can children under 18 years old, stay at the hostel?

Children under 18 must be booked in private rooms and always accompanied with adults.

How do I cancel or amend my reservation?

If you’ve booked directly with us, you can cancel up to 24 hours before arrival without charge. See our Terms & Conditions for more information.

Do you accept credit cards?

Credit cards are accepted through TAB app, which charges a 4% fee. The advantages of this app is that you won’t have to pay foreign card usage fees to your bank and it uses pure exchange rates with no hidden commission.

How do I make a group booking?

If there’s 15 or more of you who’d like to book to stay with us, please get in touch with us trough contact@nucapacha.com to handle it directly. Please note that we are not a party hostel.

Will my friends and I all be in the same room?

We do our very best to make sure you and your friends are all placed in the same room. However it also depends on our availability. If you wish to have a room for you and your friends we always recommend to book a private room instead of beds in the dorm.

What’s the difference between a twin room and a double room?

A twin room is a room that has one bunk bed and a double room is a room that has one double bed.

What time is check in and check out?

Check in is from 2:00pm. Our Receptions are open 24/7, so if you’re arriving late, just let us know! If you arrive before 2:00pm, feel free to leave your bag in the free luggage room and get out and explore the city! If you’re leaving any valuables, make sure to rent a secure locker. Please keep in mind that, as a hostel, our rooms are rented by the night rather than the day, so 5:00am is still considered to be the previous night and you’ll be charge full price. If you are planing on arriving before 7am we recommend you to book the night before. You’ll need to be packed up and out of your room by 12pm on the day that you’re leaving. Please drop your key back at Reception – other guests can use it after you!

Do you provide bed linen and towels?

A pillow and fresh bedding are provided for all rooms. Towels are provided under a small [refundable] deposit. You can ask for it at the reception.

Can I store my bags for a little while after check-out?

If you aren’t leaving the city until later in the day, you can leave your bag in the free luggage room and carry on exploring, you are welcome to hang around our facilities until 7pm. Please take this into consideration for booking your flight/bus.

Can I bring alcohol into the hostel?

We are sorry but due to our license, we can not allowed our guests to bring alcohol inside our premises. You are welcome to buy it here or head down to one of the bars in the area. If you bring alcohol into the hostel, the alcohol will be confiscated and you’ll be fined. Your alcohol will be returned to you when you leave the hostel

Can I smoke/vape in the hostels?

You’re welcome to smoke/vape in outside areas of the hostel. we have plenty, just make sure you don’t do throw cigarette butts in the flowerbeds; plants hate that. Also, would it be too much to ask you not to smoke when there are kids near you?

What's for breakfast?

As a thank you for staying with us, a complimentary breakfast including toast, eggs, natural juice and fresh brew coffee is served from 7:30 to 10:00 am.

Where can I park?

We have space for 2 cars in our building. [We do not offer parking for buses, please ensure that drivers are fully aware of this and have organised proper parking for buses prior to arrival]. If for some reason, both parking spots are taken, you can park in the street.

Can guests use the kitchen?

For sure! Our guest kitchen is open from 11am to 10pm. It is equipped with an oven, gas stove, fridges and a selection of pots, pans and dishes for cooking. Please wash up your dishes after use, so that they are available for other guests.

Can I volunteer?

Yes! We offer a range of opportunities for volunteers. Read more on our volunteering page

TERMS & CONDITIONS - GROUP GUIDELINES

These T&Cs and groups guidelines exist to make sure that your group and Nucapacha enter into a relationship of mutual understanding and to make sure that Nucapacha is a safe, happy and welcoming place to stay. By confirming a group reservation at Hostel Nucapacha , regardless if this sheet is signed, you are agreeing to the above important points.

Arrivals and Departures

  • Check-in is from 2:30pm. If you arrive before then we have a handy luggage room – dump your bags and go explore! Check-out is by 12pm so please make sure you have cleared the room by that time. Luggage storage (free until 7pm) can be arranged with reception. If you’re late to check out then you’ll be asked to pay for another night.
  • For arrivals before 7am please book the night before. Our rooms are rented by the night rather than the day; meaning 5:00am is still considered to be the previous night and you’ll be charge full price.
  • Once you have checkout, you are welcome to stay at the hostel only until 7pm.

Identification

Everyone staying at Nucapacha need to bring a valid photo identification for checking in. Preferably a passport, government issued I.D. card or driving license.

Age Restrictions

  • Bookings cannot be accepted from any person under the age of 18 unless they are accompanied by an adult (18 or over). Guests who wish to bring their children under 18 years of age must book a private room.
  • Children under 5 year old free. Children older than 5 years old full payment.

Room allocation

  • If you’re travelling with friends or family, we’ll do our utmost to book you in a room together – but this can’t always be guaranteed. If you want to make sure that you aren’t separated from your group, we’d suggest booking a private room.
  • Please note that all our dorms are mixed unless a girls only dorm is booked

Payment

  • Please be aware that we only accept Mastercard and Visa card payments for online bookings. We do not accept American Express, Diners Club cards or any other card. Cash is only accepted for walk-in bookings.
  • The total price of your reservation may be charged in full on your credit card by Nucapacha Travel S.A any time after booking.

Cancellation and Refund Policy.

  • If you wish to cancel or amend your reservation to receive a full refund you will need to notify us by email at contact@nucapacha.com 24 hours in advance of your arrival date by 2.30pm. If you notify us any less than 24 hours before your arrival date, or in case of a no-show, you will not be entitled to a refund.
  • When booking more than 3 rooms, cancellation fee applies for cancellation made less than 72 hours prior to the arrival date.
  • If you arrive a day late (or before) without informing the hostel, we will try and find you a bed, but this may not be in the same room type booked and may incur extra charges.
  • Any payments made through a third party or an online travel agency will not be refunded by us. Please make sure you check their refund policies.
  • In the event of Force Majeure (governmental action, transportation interruption, acts of God…) affecting your booking, Nucapacha is unable to refund customers and we reserve the right to receive any outstanding payments in connection to your confirmed booking.

Room Key Cards

  • You’ll be given a key when you check-in for private rooms which gives you access your room. If you lose your key, it’s USD$10 for a replacement – so please keep it safe!

Guest Behaviour

  • The comfort, safety and security of our guests is really important to us. Any guest(s) who, in the opinion of our staff, compromise the comfort, safety or security of other guests, staff or local residents will be asked to leave the hostel premises and will not be given a refund.
  • Guests are expected to respect other guests as well as staff, local residents and property.
  • Only registered guests are allowed within our premises. Visitors are only allowed in the reception area until 7pm. Please notify the reception if you are having a visitor before they arrive.

Smoking

  • Smoking is not permitted in closed areas. Any guest found smoking inside the rooms, outside of the designated areas, will be fined and asked to leave the premises immediately. A refund will not be issued for the stay.

No Drugs

  • Drug use is illegal in Ecuador. Any guest found using drugs within the hostel will be fined and asked to leave the premises immediately. A refund will not be issued.

Alcohol consumption

  • We will not serve alcohol to anyone under the age of 18.
  • Due to our hostel license, guests cannot bring their own alcohol onto the premises.
  • If you are found entering the hostel with alcohol you will be charged a fee and all alcohol will be confiscated until you leave the building at checkout. Alcohol is available in the bar and can be consumed there and in the surrounding designated areas only.

Personal Property

Each pod bed (in dorm rooms) has a personal locker for valuables. It is a guest’s responsibility to ensure that personal belongings are secure at all times. Nucapacha hostels accept no liability for the loss, theft or damage to guests’ property, however sustained or caused. We strongly recommend guests bring a padlock to use on luggage.

Damage to Hostel Nucapacha’s Property

  • Guests will be expected to reimburse Nucapacha Hostel for any damage caused by inappropriate behaviour during their stay.

Your Information

Nucapacha Hostel will only store and use information you supply to us or which is supplied to us for the purposes of carrying out a contract with you, and to inform you of other services and offers which we make available from time to time. If you do not wish to receive such information please let us know by emailing us at: contact@nucapacha.com

Discretion

  • All bookings are subject to availability and Nucapacha Hostel reserve the right to decline any booking at its discretion.

General

  • We accept groups with guests from the age of 12+.
  • A USD$100 [refundable] guarantee will be needed to time of check-in to make sure our T&C will be enforce by your group. Also for any damages that may be caused. It will be returned in full if it no inconvenience has been submitted.
  • We do not offer parking, please ensure that drivers are fully aware of this and have organised proper parking for buses prior to arrival.
  • Due to the layout of the buildings and the room types chosen, we can’t always accommodate groups on the same floor. We will, however, do our best to place them as close together as possible.
  • We must receive a list for each group at least 1 week prior arrival. This list must include the names of the guests dates of births and passport numbers.
  • We offer breakfast from 730am to 10am – as such it can get busy in the mornings! We work on a first come, first served basis, so please get in touch as soon as possible to get served. If your group is larger than 20pp and are leaving before 8:30 am please let us know in order to organize a packed breakfast as we won’t be able to serve your group with such a short time slot.

Arrival/departure

  • Check-in time is from 2.30pm.
  • Check-out is until 12 pm. If the rooms are not vacated by that time, a fine will be applied.
  • Groups are required to check-in and check-out all together. If this isn’t possible, please advise the Reservations Team in advance.
  • We offer free luggage storage before check-in and after check-out.
  • Reception is open 24/7 so there is always a member of the team to help you and your group with any questions / issues.
  • For arrivals before 7am please book the night before. Our rooms are rented by the night rather than the day; meaning 5:00 am is still considered to be the previous night and you’ll be charge full price.
  • Once you have checkout, you are welcome to stay at the hostel only until 7pm.
  • We will accommodate the number of people in the room types pre-confirmed by you via email. If more people arrive, they will be asked to pay the available rate at check-in.
  • A ‘Group Deposit’ of USD$100 must be completed in cash at the time of arrival. this deposit will be returned at checkout, however, money will be deducted if any of the house rules are not respected or if any damages have occurred. A copy of the rules can be sent at request, and will be provided on arrival.
  • We are unable to check-in a group until the rules have been signed and the Group Deposit form has been filled in by the group leader.

In house

  • Due to our license, guests are not allowed to bring alcohol into the hostels or drink alcohol bought from outside. Please come and enjoy the bar (over 18’s only) if you’d like to drink. Any guests found with alcohol from elsewhere may be fined and all alcohol will be confiscated until you leave the building at checkout.

  • Please make sure guests have adequate travel insurance. We can’t take responsibility for any lost or stolen property, nor accidents.

Cancellation policy

  • If a whole reservation is cancelled up to 20 days before the arrival date, the accommodation deposit (50% off the full cost) will not be refunded.
  • If only a portion of the beds are cancelled up to 15 days before the arrival date, 25% of the cost of the beds cancelled will be charged as a cancellation fee, and cannot be used against the remainder of the accommodation balance.
  • If you are booking within 20 days of your arrival date, no refund will be given for any cancellation or amendments, and the full amount will be due.
  • In the event of Force Majeure (governmental action, transportation interruption, terrorist attack, acts of God…) affecting your booking, we reserve the right to receive any outstanding payments in connection to your confirmed booking.

Payment
